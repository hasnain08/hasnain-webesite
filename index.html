About Me

I am currently a fourth year Kinesiology & Physical Education student at the University of Toronto St George. My passion for a healthy lifestyle and weightlifting from a young age has inspired me to become a Certified Personal Trainer. My goal is to help people become a better version of their selves by educating them in proper nutrition and teaching them to move effectively and efficiently. My academic and professional experience has helped a diverse group of population reach their goals in safe and timely manner. My student experience as an assistant Exercise Therapist at the Cardiac Rehab Program has given me the skills and knowledge to help patients with cardiovascular diseases. I continuously strive to gain more knowledge and experience in healthcare and fitness industry.Feel free to contact me or just say hi on social media.
